Vivekanda Institute of Human Excellence

The chief aim of the center is to instill faith in oneself and impart nobler values of life and life-giving strength, to raise individuals to higher levels of strength and felicity with spiritual, moral, ethical and eternal values of personality development and human excellence. Various programmes are intended for the aspirants coming from different cross sections of society, like the youth, students, teachers, employees of Government departments, public sector undertakings.

Today ‘we require that education’, in swami Vivekananda’s own words, ‘by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased and by which one can stand on one’s own feet.’

A new trust and inspiration has to be given to the youth in various educational institutions, irrespective of the background. The youth have to be trained to discover their own hidden potential and help them convert it to “Excellent Performance ”. For this, each youngster has to develop a healthy self-image of oneself. As the great writer Emerson says, “What lies before you and what lies behind you are tiny matters compared to what lies within you and you are the only one who can use your own ability. It is an awesome responsibility.” The potential of an average person is like a huge ocean un-sailed, a new continent unexplored, a world of possibilities waiting to be released and channeled towards some great good.

Such training is being provided at the vivekananda personality development center. Youngsters from in and around Huzurnagar participate in the inspiring and motivating sessions on:

  • Yoga
  • Meditation
  • Dance and Music classes
  • Self confidence and Self discipline
  • Career and Character Development
  • Emotional stability
  • Leadership qualities
  • Indian cultural values
  • Spirit of service
  • Devotional singing
  • The lives of great leaders
  • Personlaity development
  • Communication

Also Free tuition classes are conducted for the poor children in Our Vivekananda Bala vikas.

All the youth studying in Senior Secondary Schools and colleges are welcome to be the members of this dynamic youth group and get inspired and help others get inspired.
